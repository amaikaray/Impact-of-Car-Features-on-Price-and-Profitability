# 🚗 Impact of Car Features on Price and Profitability

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the relationship between various car features and their impact on vehicle pricing and profitability. Using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), regression analysis, and interactive dashboards, the project examines how technical specifications, market categories, fuel efficiency, and brand characteristics influence a car's Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (MSRP).

Microsoft Excel was used to clean, analyze, and visualize the dataset, helping identify the factors that drive pricing decisions and consumer demand. The insights generated can support manufacturers in optimizing product development and pricing strategies to maximize profitability.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Automobile manufacturers operate in a highly competitive market where balancing customer demand with profitability is essential. Understanding which vehicle features influence pricing and consumer preferences enables manufacturers to make informed product development and pricing decisions.

The objective of this project is to analyze the relationship between car specifications, market categories, and MSRP to identify the key factors affecting vehicle pricing and support strategic business decisions.

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing

Before performing the analysis, the dataset was prepared to ensure accuracy and consistency by carrying out the following preprocessing steps:

* Examined the dataset structure and variables.
* Identified and handled missing values.
* Removed duplicate records where applicable.
* Standardized categorical values for consistency.
* Verified numerical data for completeness.
* Prepared the dataset for statistical analysis and dashboard creation.

The dataset contained over **11,000 car models** with information related to vehicle specifications, fuel efficiency, pricing, and market categories.

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### A. Market Category & Popularity Analysis

* Analyzed the popularity of car models across different market categories.
* Created Pivot Tables to summarize market-wise popularity.
* Developed combination charts to visualize category-wise trends.

### B. Engine Power vs. Price Analysis

* Examined the relationship between engine horsepower and MSRP.
* Created scatter plots with trendlines to identify pricing patterns.

### C. Regression Analysis

Regression analysis was performed to determine which vehicle features have the greatest influence on MSRP.

The analysis evaluated factors such as:

* Engine Horsepower
* Highway MPG
* City MPG
* Engine Cylinders
* Vehicle Size
* Market Category
* Popularity

### D. Manufacturer Price Analysis

* Compared the average MSRP across different automobile manufacturers.
* Created Pivot Tables and bar charts to identify premium and budget brands.

### E. Fuel Efficiency Analysis

* Investigated the relationship between engine cylinders and highway fuel efficiency.
* Calculated correlation coefficients to measure the strength of the relationship.

### F. Interactive Dashboard

An interactive dashboard was developed using Pivot Charts and Slicers to analyze:

* Car price distribution by brand and body style.
* Average MSRP across manufacturers.
* Impact of transmission type on vehicle pricing.
* Fuel efficiency trends by model year and body style.
* Relationship between horsepower, fuel efficiency, and MSRP across brands.

## 📖 Key Insights

* Premium market categories generally exhibited higher average vehicle prices.
* Engine horsepower showed a positive relationship with MSRP, indicating that more powerful vehicles tend to command higher prices.
* Fuel efficiency decreased as the number of engine cylinders increased, reflecting a negative correlation between these variables.
* Luxury and performance brands consistently recorded higher average MSRPs than mass-market manufacturers.
* Market category, engine specifications, and vehicle features were identified as significant factors influencing vehicle pricing.
* Interactive dashboards enabled dynamic comparison of pricing trends across manufacturers, body styles, and model years.
